Welcome to Rexy Curling! 🥌

How to Play:

  • Move your cursor over point A to pick up the stone
  • Move smoothly toward point B — stone follows your cursor exactly
  • Stone drops if you stop for 1 second OR deviate more than 90°
  • 3 stones per round — best stone score counts for that round
  • Stones can nudge each other if you collide! Use it wisely
  • Complete 10 rounds — smooth control is key!
