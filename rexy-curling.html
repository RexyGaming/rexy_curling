Welcome to Rexy Curling! 🥌

How to Play:

  • Move your hand cursor over point A to pick up the stone
  • Once holding the stone, move smoothly toward point B
  • The stone drops if you stop for 1 second OR deviate more than 90° from your committed direction
  • Get as close to point B as possible for maximum points
  • Complete 10 rounds - smooth control is key!
